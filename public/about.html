About Us

Time, like our lives, is precious. How many resumes have you had to discard because it didn't match company expectations? Companies receive numerous amounts of resumes from events like hackathons and career fairs every week. Why spend more time and effort to review resumes? Our system will revolutionize resume analysis.

Recruiters and hiring managers alike waste time reviewing hundrends, possibly thousands of resumes they receive. With RecruitBot, resume reviews are customized and analyzed in a second. You can weigh more or less the characteristics that is important to you: GPA, experience, etc. when considering intern and full time candidates. Our innovative system will save you time in eliminating unqualified candidates to find top talent.
